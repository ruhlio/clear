module Clear

Overview

Welcome to Clear ORM !

Clear ORM is currently in heavy development. The goal is to provide an advanced ORM for postgreSQL.

Instead of working on adapter for differents database, I wanted to offer the maximum features for a specific platform.

It's not every day we chose a database layout, and there's few reasons for you to change your database during the development cycle (at least, from SQL to SQL).

Postgres offers a lot of features in a very performant engine, and seems suitable for large projects in Crystal.

And here you have ! The ORM made for Postgres and Crystal, simple to use, full of ideas stolen to ActiveRecord and Sequel :-).
